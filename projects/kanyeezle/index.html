      How to play

      Heads up: Cruel Summer counts as an album in this version, and every track on it is in play.

      One song is chosen each day at local midnight — everyone gets the same one. Each guess is scored against the answer on four clues:

      • Green — exact match.
      • Yellow — close. An album on either side chronologically, a track number within 2, a length within 30 seconds, or at least one shared featured artist.
      • Grey — not close.

      Arrows point toward the answer. On Album, ↑ means the answer is on a later record and ↓ an earlier one. On Track and Length, ↑ means a higher track number or a longer song, ↓ lower or shorter.

      Track numbers and lengths come from MusicBrainz. Featured artists are compiled and edited from Wikipedia (CC BY-SA 4.0) and include guests the official credits leave off. Skits are excluded.
