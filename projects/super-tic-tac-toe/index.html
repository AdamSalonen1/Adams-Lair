The OG build

Super Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, but each square is its own game of tic-tac-toe. Win a small board to claim its square; line up three claimed squares to win. You play X — your opponent (O) answers at random.

New game You're X · O plays itself
How to play

The board is a 3×3 grid of small tic-tac-toe boards. Win a small board and you claim its square on the big board. Win three squares in a row on the big board and you win the whole thing.

The catch: the square you play in decides which small board your opponent must play in next. Play the top-left square of any board, and the next move happens on the top-left board. If that board is already won, the next player gets a free choice.

You're X. After every move you make, O answers on its own with a random legal move. Hit New game to start over.
